Veggie Freshness Check App

The vegetable freshness tracking system based on the machine learning is an integrated monitoring and management information system, which consists of image processing technology. Machine learning and as well as other practical high-tech techniques. The vegetables we consume provides nourishment and gives energy to our body which helps us to perform our day-to-day activities. A healthy and fresh vegetables is the most important way to keep our body healthy and energetic.

The vegetables kept at room temperature undergo rapid bacterial growth and chemical changes in them. Consuming unhealthy vegetables can cause several foodborne diseases which may harm our health. This image processing and machine learning based Android application aims to detect the freshness of vegetables and tells whether can eat it or bin it. An android application is developed to display the results about freshness of vegetables using the mobile camera.
